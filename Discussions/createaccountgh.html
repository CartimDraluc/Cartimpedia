How To Create An Account in Github?



Go to https://github.com/

Choose Sign Up

Enter your Email address


Create a password (Password must be at 15 characters OR at least 8 characters including a number and lowercase letter)

Example 1: thisisapassword (15 characters)
Example 2: EXAMPLe7 (8 characters including a number and lowercase letter)


Enter Your Username

They will ask you about Receive product updates and announcements via email. If you want to receive, enter 'y'. If not, enter 'n'.

You have to vertify your account. Click start puzzle and follow the instructions. 4 puzzles in total

After that they will send a launch code to your email.(8 characters in total).

You're Almost Done!

They will ask how many team members will be working with you and Are you a teacher or student. Choose the correct ones. Then continue.

Choose what you are interested in using. Then continue.

Choose 'Continue for Free'

And done you have Successfully create an account in Github!!!
